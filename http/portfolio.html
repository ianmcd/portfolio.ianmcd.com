Much of my time was focused on maintaining & continuing to improve our marketing websites from both a design & performance perspective.

I joined Corvisa in 2013. Initially as part of a team of two, performing a wide variety of design related tasks. We quickly grew larger and as more structured design roles were assigned I took on the responsibility of designing, developing & maintaining our marketing websites.

Having previously come from an agency, as part of the Corvisa team I learned a lot about designing within a team and focusing on a single brand. I also was given the opportunity to hone my development skills and quickly developed the ingenuity to think and work through complex problems.

I enjoyed the variety of work that came with working in an agency, but I felt far more attached working on a single brand. It was rewarding to watch Corvisa grow knowing that the work we were doing was having an impact. Working at Corvisa was truly an invaluable experience.

Corvisa was acquired by ShoreTel, Inc. in January 2016.

corvisa.com

As the primary marketing website, the aim of this project was to attract potential customers and present the complexities of our offerings in a simple, straightforward manner. We also wanted to position ourselves in a manner that set us apart from our competitors; modern, simple and design-forward.

One of the main issues we faced with corvisa.com was presenting all of our products and features in a way that was both informative and engaging without overwhelming or confusing potential customers with too much information. In the end we found succes employing the use of illustrations, animations and video in addition to scaled back text content to get major talking points across.
